It's time to democratize Fashion

In today's world, influencers create trends, inspire designers and collaborate with brands to boost sales numbers. For all of that impact, we think they get too little of the benefits

At the same time, designers are still not designing for real women's shapes and sizes

We're here to change that

We are creating a concept store where social media's hottest trend makers are in the driving seat. And we'll start with active wear, because promoting healthy living is something that we really love.

As creative directors, influencers will design their own collections - and we'll take care of manufacturing and fulfillment.

So What exactly is bodysong?

bodysong is a concept store of ethically manufactured activewear pieces designed and curated by influencers.

More than a store, it's an inclusive lifestyle community around mind:body health.

Introducing the best Manufacturing

Like you, we are passionate about where something comes from and how it was made. That's why we created an industry leading ethical and sustainable manufacturing plant in SE Asia. Each garment is made by a talented craftsman from start to finish.

Every sale through bodysong goes towards supporting the local community. And we provide full transparency into the business.

Calling all #Fitspo Influencers

We believe that influencers are the designers of the future. That's why we're looking for social media's hottest tastemakers to join bodysong as founding members.

They will design their dream activewear collection and retail it on the bodysong platform, benefiting from its growth as equal partners.
